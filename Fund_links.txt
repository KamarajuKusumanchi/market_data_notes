====== Fund links ======
Created Tuesday 25 February 2020

--------------------
To get the information on a fund, use
	https://screener.fidelity.com/ftgw/etf/goto/snapshot/keyStatistics.jhtml?symbols=<ticker>
Example:
https://screener.fidelity.com/ftgw/etf/goto/snapshot/keyStatistics.jhtml?symbols=VOO gives information on VOO.

For vanguard funds, use
	https://investor.vanguard.com/etf/profile/<ticker>
The ticker can be specified either in capitals or in lower case.

Example:
https://investor.vanguard.com/etf/profile/VOO gives information on VOO.
https://investor.vanguard.com/etf/profile/voo gives the same information.
--------------------
