====== reported date ======
Created Friday 21 January 2022

--------------------
https://www.nasdaq.com/market-activity/stocks/<ticker>/earnings shows the reported date of earnings for a given ticker for the past 4 quarters.

Ex:- https://www.nasdaq.com/market-activity/stocks/cost/earnings
